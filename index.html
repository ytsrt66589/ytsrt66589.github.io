Yen-Tung (Arthur) Yeh

Master in Graduate Institute of Communication Engineering, National Tawian University

I’m Yen-Tung Yeh. I’m currently the first-year master student in Graduate Institute of Communication Engineering at National Taiwan University. I am a lab member in Music and AI Lab, advised by by Prof. Yi-Hsuan Yang. My research interest focused on interlligent music production, including audio effect modeling, virtual analog modeling, automatic mixing, and audio generation.

I am a research intern at the Positive Grid for Spring 2024, focusing on neural amplifier modeling. Also, I’m the collaborator with Taipei Music Center (mentored by Bo-Yu Chen), working on LLM-based music production oriented understanding.
