I'm Yen-Tung Yeh. I'm currently the first-year PhD student in [Graduate Institute of Communication Engineering](https://comm.ntu.edu.tw/en/) at [National Taiwan University](https://www.ntu.edu.tw/english/). I am a lab member in 
[Music and AI Lab](https://affige.github.io/lab.html), advised by by Prof. [Yi-Hsuan Yang](https://affige.github.io/). My research interest focused on interlligent music production, including **audio effect modeling**, **virtual analog modeling**, **automatic mixing/mastering***. 

Previously, I was research intern at the [Sony AI](https://ai.sony/) advised by [Junghyun (Tony) Koo](https://www.linkedin.com/in/junghyun-koo-525a31251/?originalSubdomain=kr) during Autumn 2024. Our research project is about audio effect representation learning. I completed research internship at the [Positive Grid](https://www.positivegrid.com/) during Spring 2024, where I focused on neural amplifier modeling. Additionally, I maintain an ongoing collaboration with [Taipei Music Center](https://www.tmc.taipei/), where I work on LLM-based music production understanding with my colleagues [Bo-Yu Chen](https://scholar.google.com/citations?user=ydsKndkAAAAJ&hl=zh-TW&authuser=1).
