I'm Yen-Tung Yeh. I'm currently the first-year master student in [Graduate Institute of Communication Engineering](https://comm.ntu.edu.tw/en/) at [National Taiwan University](https://www.ntu.edu.tw/english/). I am a lab member in 
[Music and AI Lab](https://affige.github.io/lab.html), advised by by Prof. [Yi-Hsuan Yang](https://affige.github.io/). My research interest focused on interlligent music production, including **audio effect modeling**, **virtual analog modeling**, **automatic mixing**, and **audio generation**. 

I am a research intern at the [Positive Grid](https://www.positivegrid.com/) for Spring 2024, focusing on neural amplifier modeling. Also, I'm the collaborator with [Taipei Music Center](https://www.tmc.taipei/) (mentored by [Bo-Yu Chen](https://scholar.google.com/citations?user=ydsKndkAAAAJ&hl=zh-TW&authuser=1)), working on LLM-based music production oriented understanding.
